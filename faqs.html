Getting Started

What is Spreadr? How does it work?
Spreadr imports Amazon products into Shopify and helps monetize via affiliate links or dropshipping workflows.
Is Spreadr free to try?
Yes. Every plan includes a 7-day free trial with no credit card required.
Do I need an Amazon Associates account?
You need Amazon Associates for affiliate mode. Dropshipping workflows can run independently.

Import & Sync

How do I import Amazon products?
Paste an Amazon URL or ASIN, bulk import through CSV, or use the Chrome Extension.
Can I bulk import and bulk sync products?
Yes. Spreadr supports bulk import and bulk sync actions on higher plans.
What does “Super-Sync” mean?
Super-Sync provides real-time updates for price and availability to reduce overselling risk.

Affiliate & Dropshipping

Can I use affiliate mode and dropshipping mode together?
Yes, merchants can run both modes and switch based on product strategy.
Do I keep 100% of my affiliate commissions?
Yes. Spreadr does not take any commission cut.

Pricing & Billing

What plans are available?
$5 / $10 / $20 / $35 monthly plans are available.
Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?
Yes, plan changes can be made as your store grows.

Technical & Support

Does Spreadr support all Amazon regions?
Yes, all major Amazon regions are supported with geo-localization.

Use the contact page or email support@spreadr.freshdesk.com.
