Getting Started

What is Spreadr and how does it work?
Spreadr is a Shopify app that imports Amazon products and supports affiliate or dropshipping workflows.
Is Spreadr free to try?
Yes. All plans include a 7-day free trial and no credit card is required to start.
Do I need an Amazon Associates account?
For affiliate mode yes. Dropshipping workflows can run independently.

Import & Sync

How do I import products?
Paste URL/ASIN, upload CSV, or use the Chrome Extension.
What is Super-Sync?
Real-time product price and availability synchronization with Amazon data.
Can I bulk import and bulk sync?
Yes, supported on applicable plans with manual and automated options.

Pricing & Billing

What plans are available?
Basic $5, Pro $10, Gold $20, Higher $35 per month.
Can I change my plan later?
Yes, upgrades and downgrades are supported.
